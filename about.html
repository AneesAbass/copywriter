This isn’t your average tale of a boy who had big dreams of becoming a writer

I wasn't always reading books or writing my own travel blogs. I don’t have a journalism degree, and I didn’t win any storytelling awards at school — although I was jealous of the kids who did.

In fact, I didn’t even know I could write.

I was always artsy fartsy, lived for media studies class and had my rock music blaring headphones super-glued on instead of my face buried in a book.

After nearly a decade working as a graphic designer and finished artist, I realised the work I was doing made me feel like I wasn’t making the world a better place. Product packaging full of plastic, advertising for consumer products and concepts that felt unoriginal or ‘nothing special’. I was so burnt out that I craved the simple life. So, I swapped the paintbrush for the apron and got a job at a chocolate shop.

It was here that I realised I was a selling star. I enjoyed conversing with customers, building relationships, describing products and helping them select special gifts. Closing the sale was fun and rewarding — seeing them get so excited to rip open that bag of chocolate almonds before they even made it out the door.

I felt like I was actually helping people. It wasn’t just chocolate. It was memories. It was joy. It was quality. It was happiness.

But then, the pandemic happened — that old chestnut

I fell down a rabbit hole of online learning and became obsessed with creative copywriting services, tone of voice and brand messaging. Like, I mean obsessed — I couldn’t even sleep because I was so happy that I found something I was head over heels for. So, one day, I was like, “I could totally have my own freelance business.” And that was that.

I love infusing my creative mind with my sales and customer service skills to give growing businesses the words that sell in the authentic and ethical way that’s true to them.

Today, I work with brands and businesses that want to connect with their audience on a deeper level, be memorable and make their mark. Because they have something meaningful to share and something purposeful to do. I’m here to help get it out there!

I’ve helped clients in the e-commerce, health and wellness, pet health and creative services space feel more confident in their words and communicate their message to their dream audience — clearer than ever before.

Just like you, I’m building a business I’m passionate about where I can help people. Giving them positive transformations and a voice to be heard so that they can help others, too.

Thank you for hopping aboard this journey with me.
