FZSMART NEWS — YOUTUBE SCRIPT WRITING

But their scripts? They were heavy. Too formal. More like a report than a conversation.

THE PROJECT

When FZSmart News reached out, their main challenge was clear: great information, weak delivery.

My task was to rewrite their YouTube scripts so that:

    Headlines became hooks.
    Stories flowed like conversations.
    Calls-to-action felt natural, not forced.

The goal wasn’t just to inform. It was to inform, entertain, and retain.

Before

After

MY WORDS: Hooks + Phrases

“Here’s the part they don’t tell you on the evening news…”
“Let’s break this down without the jargon.”
“Imagine this happening in your own street…”
“Stay with me — because the next detail will surprise you.”
“This isn’t just a headline. It’s a warning sign.”
“Before you scroll away, here’s why this matters to you.”
“News moves fast. We make sure you don’t just hear it — you get it.”

THE OUTCOME

With their new scripts, FZSmart News saw:

  • ✅ Longer watch times thanks to engaging hooks.
  • ✅ Higher subscriber growth because content felt approachable.
  • ✅ A stronger brand voice that stood out from generic, robotic news copy.
Today, FZSmart News doesn’t just share updates. They spark curiosity, invite discussion, and keep audiences coming back for more.
